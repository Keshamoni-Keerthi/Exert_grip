Herbal Remedies for Digestive Disorders

Peppermint (Mentha piperita)

Benefits:

  • Relieves indigestion, bloating, and gas.
  • Soothes the stomach lining and reduces symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).
  • Contains menthol, which relaxes the muscles of the gastrointestinal tract.

Usage:

  • Drink peppermint tea after meals for better digestion.
  • Use peppermint oil capsules for IBS and digestive discomfort.

Ginger (Zingiber officinale)

Benefits:

  • Stimulates digestion by increasing enzyme activity.
  • Reduces nausea, vomiting, and motion sickness.
  • Helps relieve gas, bloating, and stomach cramps.

Usage:

  • Boil fresh ginger slices to make tea and drink it warm.
  • Consume grated ginger with a little honey before meals to boost digestion.
