Skin Disease Treatment with Ayurvedic Plants

Here are two medicinal plants known for their healing properties to treat common skin conditions.

1. Hibiscus (Hibiscus rosa-sinensis)

Benefits:

  • Promotes hair growth by strengthening roots and improving blood circulation to the scalp.
  • Reduces hair fall and prevents premature graying.
  • Moisturizes the scalp and hair, preventing dryness and dandruff.

Usage:

  • Crush hibiscus flowers and leaves into a paste and apply as a hair mask.
  • Boil hibiscus flowers in coconut oil to make a hair oil for regular use.

2. Fenugreek (Trigonella foenum-graecum)

Benefits:

  • Rich in proteins and nicotinic acid, which strengthen hair follicles and encourage growth.
  • Reduces dandruff and soothes an itchy scalp.
  • Adds shine and softness to the hair.

Usage:

  • Soak fenugreek seeds overnight, grind into a paste, and apply as a hair mask.
  • Use fenugreek water (strained from soaked seeds) as a hair rinse.
