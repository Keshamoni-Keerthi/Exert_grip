Ayurvedic Recipes for Skin Disease Treatment

Aloe Vera

Benefits:

  • Soothes and hydrates the skin, reducing dryness.
  • Contains antioxidants, vitamins A, C, and E, promoting skin repair and reducing inflammation.
  • Helps heal wounds, burns, and acne due to its antibacterial properties.

Usage:

  • Apply the gel directly to the skin as a moisturizer or to treat specific areas of irritation or sunburn.
  • Use in DIY face masks for hydration and glow.

Turmeric (Curcuma longa)

Benefits:

  • Contains curcumin, a potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant that reduces redness and irritation.
  • Helps with acne, pigmentation, and skin brightening.
  • Promotes wound healing and fights off bacteria that can cause skin issues.

Usage:

  • Mix turmeric powder with honey or yogurt for a face mask.
  • Use turmeric-infused oils for massages or skincare routines.
