Ayurvedic Plants for Liver Health

Milk Thistle (Silybum marianum)

Benefits:

  • Contains silymarin, a compound that protects liver cells from toxins and promotes regeneration.
  • Helps reduce inflammation and oxidative stress in the liver.
  • Supports liver detoxification and improves its overall function.

Usage:

  • Take milk thistle supplements (capsules or extracts) as directed.
  • Use powdered milk thistle seeds to make tea.

Turmeric (Curcuma longa) for Liver Health

Benefits:

  • Contains curcumin, a potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory compound that protects the liver.
  • Stimulates bile production, aiding in fat digestion and liver detoxification.
  • Prevents liver damage from toxins and supports recovery from fatty liver disease.

Usage:

  • Add turmeric powder to food or drinks (e.g., golden milk).
  • Take turmeric supplements or extract as advised by a healthcare provider.
