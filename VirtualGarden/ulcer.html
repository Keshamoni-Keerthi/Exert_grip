Herbal Remedies for Soothing Ulcers

Aloe Vera (Aloe barbadensis miller)

Benefits:

  • Contains compounds that help reduce inflammation and promote healing of the stomach lining.
  • Soothes irritated tissues and provides a cooling effect to reduce pain.
  • Helps in reducing acid reflux and can aid in ulcer healing.

Usage:

  • Drink aloe vera juice (preferably with no added sugar) to soothe the stomach.
  • Apply aloe vera gel topically for any external ulcers or sores (such as mouth ulcers).

Licorice Root (Glycyrrhiza glabra)

Benefits:

  • Contains glycyrrhizin, which has anti-inflammatory properties that can help protect the stomach lining and reduce ulcer symptoms.
  • Promotes the production of mucus that helps protect the stomach lining from acid.
  • Supports healing and reduces irritation caused by ulcers.

Usage:

  • Drink licorice root tea to soothe the stomach and promote healing.
  • Use deglycyrrhizinated licorice (DGL) supplements if you are concerned about the side effects of glycyrrhizin.
