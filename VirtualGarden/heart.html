Ayurvedic Plants for Heart Health

Discover two potent medicinal plants that can support heart health and help prevent cardiovascular diseases.

1. Arjuna (Terminalia arjuna)

Benefits:

  • Strengthens the heart muscles and improves cardiac function.
  • Helps manage blood pressure and cholesterol levels.
  • Contains antioxidants that reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the cardiovascular system.

Usage:

  • Take Arjuna bark powder with water or honey daily.
  • Consume as a herbal tea by boiling Arjuna bark in water.

2. Garlic (Allium sativum)

Benefits:

  • Reduces cholesterol levels and helps prevent plaque buildup in arteries.
  • Lowers blood pressure and improves overall blood circulation.
  • Contains allicin, which has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that benefit heart health.

Usage:

  • Consume 1-2 raw garlic cloves daily for maximum benefits.
  • Add garlic to meals or take garlic supplements (as advised by a healthcare provider).

Both plants are powerful natural remedies for maintaining a healthy heart and preventing cardiovascular diseases. However, consult a healthcare professional before integrating these into your routine, especially if you're on heart-related medications.
