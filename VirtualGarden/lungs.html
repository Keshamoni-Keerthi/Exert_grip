Ayurvedic Plants for Lung Health

Tulsi (Holy Basil, Ocimum sanctum)

Benefits:

  • Acts as a natural expectorant, helping to clear mucus from the lungs.
  • Contains anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties that reduce respiratory infections.
  • Strengthens the respiratory system and eases symptoms of asthma and bronchitis.

Usage:

  • Brew tulsi leaves as tea for daily consumption.
  • Inhale steam infused with tulsi leaves to relieve congestion.

Licorice Root (Glycyrrhiza glabra)

Benefits:

  • Soothes the respiratory tract and reduces inflammation in the lungs.
  • Helps loosen mucus, making it easier to expel from the airways.
  • Provides relief from sore throat, cough, and lung irritation caused by pollution or infections.

Usage:

  • Prepare licorice root tea by boiling the root in water.
  • Take licorice root powder with honey as a natural remedy for cough.
