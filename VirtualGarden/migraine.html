Herbal Remedies for Migraines

Peppermint (Mentha piperita)

Benefits:

  • Helps relieve headache pain by increasing blood flow to the affected area.
  • Has a cooling effect that can soothe tension headaches and migraines.
  • Relieves nausea often associated with migraines.

Usage:

  • Apply diluted peppermint oil (mixed with a carrier oil) to the temples and massage gently.
  • Drink peppermint tea or inhale steam infused with peppermint oil.

Feverfew (Tanacetum parthenium)

Benefits:

  • Reduces the frequency and intensity of migraine attacks by inhibiting inflammatory substances.
  • Helps reduce pain and inflammation associated with migraines.
  • Provides relief from nausea and sensitivity to light during migraines.

Usage:

  • Take feverfew supplements (capsules or tablets) regularly for migraine prevention.
  • You can also use feverfew leaves in tea or as a tincture.
