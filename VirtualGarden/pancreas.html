Ayurvedic Plants for Pancreas Health

Bitter Gourd (Momordica charantia)

Benefits:

  • Helps regulate blood sugar levels by improving insulin sensitivity.
  • Stimulates pancreatic function and may support beta cell regeneration.
  • Reduces inflammation and oxidative stress in the pancreas.

Usage:

  • Drink fresh bitter gourd juice daily on an empty stomach.
  • Add bitter gourd to your diet as a cooked vegetable.

Gudmar (Gymnema sylvestre)

Benefits:

  • Known as the "sugar destroyer," it helps regulate blood glucose by promoting insulin secretion.
  • Supports the pancreas by reducing strain on beta cells.
  • May assist in repairing and regenerating pancreatic cells.

Usage:

  • Consume Gudmar leaves as tea.
  • Take Gudmar supplements in capsule or powder form as directed by a healthcare professional.
